ABOUT

xLoadIt - Manage your files

xLoadIt is a file hosting service that allows you to upload, store, download and share your files.

The service offers useful file sharing options by generating links. Free users are able to download any type of files using a shared link.

Registering to the service unlocks a host of features. You can have access to a file manager that helps you track uploads and manage files added to your xLoadIt account. You can sort through all types of files with ease and organize them in folders. Therefore, no matter where you go, you can take your files with you. Furthermore, you get the option to create a sharing link while uploading the file or folder. All files and folders you share from your xLoadIt account, will be accessible by anyone with the shared URL.

xLoadIt is an affordable service, you can switch to a paid account anytime you want for more storage options. Check our pricing plan here.
